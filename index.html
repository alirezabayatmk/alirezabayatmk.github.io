Hi! I'm a PhD student at the UKP Lab, Technical University of Darmstadt, advised by Prof. Iryna Gurevych. I work on mechanistic interpretability of large language models: circuit discovery, activation patching, and causal interventions, with a focus on the methodological reliability of the structures we find and how they relate to model behavior. My broader research interests are listed on my CV.

Before Darmstadt I did my M.Sc. at Leibniz University Hannover, working on AutoML for parameter-efficient fine-tuning with Prof. Marius Lindauer's group, and co-authored an ACL 2024 paper on argumentation rewriting with Prof. Henning Wachsmuth's group. I've also spent time in industry (Vodafone, Astyage, and co-founding Gecko).

You can find my published work on the publications page.

Feel free to email me about my research! I'm open to collaboration and exchanging research ideas, and happy to chat about careers in AI/NLP.
